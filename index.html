  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)

      4. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until the garlic is golden brown and fragrant
